About Living Guidelines

Our guidelines are continuously updated as new evidence emerges, ensuring clinicians always have access to current, high-quality recommendations.

Evidence-Based

Developed using GRADE methodology with systematic reviews of the best available evidence.

Continuously Updated

Regular surveillance ensures recommendations reflect the latest research findings.

Expert Panels
