Our Approach to Managing Conflicts of Interest

The Australian Living Guideline for the Pharmacological Management of Inflammatory Arthritis is committed to ensuring that our recommendations are developed free from undue influence. We recognise that conflicts of interest are common among experts in any clinical field, and our approach focuses on transparency and appropriate management rather than exclusion.

All panel members are required to declare their interests prior to participating in guideline development. These declarations are assessed using a standardised matrix, and appropriate management strategies are implemented for any identified conflicts. Declarations are updated regularly and made publicly available to ensure ongoing transparency.
