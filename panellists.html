Guideline Panel Members

Meet the expert clinicians, researchers, and consumer representatives who develop our evidence-based recommendations.

About Our Panel

The Australian Living Guideline is developed by a multidisciplinary panel of expert clinicians, researchers, and consumer representatives. Panel members are selected based on their expertise relevant to each specific recommendation topic.

JS
Prof Jane Smith
Rheumatologist
University Hospital, Sydney

Prof Smith is a consultant rheumatologist with over 20 years of experience in the management of inflammatory arthritis. Her research focuses on optimising DMARD therapy and treat-to-target strategies.

Panel Chair RA Expert
Recommendations: Initial DMARD therapy, MTX route of administration, Dose reduction
RJ
Dr Robert Jones
Evidence Team
Cochrane Musculoskeletal, Melbourne

Dr Jones leads systematic reviews for the guideline and provides methodological expertise in evidence synthesis. He specialises in network meta-analysis and GRADE methodology.

Methodologist Evidence Lead
Recommendations: All recommendations (evidence review)
MW
Maria Williams
Consumer
Arthritis Australia

Maria brings the patient perspective to guideline development. Living with rheumatoid arthritis for 15 years, she advocates for recommendations that reflect patient values and preferences.

Consumer Voice
Recommendations: Initial DMARD therapy, Dose reduction, Perioperative management
AC
Dr Amanda Clarke
GP
Brisbane Medical Centre

Dr Clarke is a general practitioner with a special interest in musculoskeletal medicine. She provides primary care perspective on shared care arrangements and monitoring protocols.

Primary Care
Recommendations: Laboratory monitoring, Shared care
SL
Prof Steven Lee
Orthopaedic Surgeon
Royal Adelaide Hospital

Prof Lee specialises in joint replacement surgery for patients with inflammatory arthritis. He contributes expertise on perioperative DMARD management.

Surgical Expert
Recommendations: Perioperative DMARD management
PT
Dr Priya Patel
Pharmacist
Alfred Health, Melbourne

Dr Patel is a clinical pharmacist specialising in rheumatology. She provides expertise on drug interactions, safety monitoring, and medication management.

Medication Safety
Recommendations: Laboratory monitoring, Drug interactions
